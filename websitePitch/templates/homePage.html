This project is to help eliviate the issue
we have with the parking here at UB

As it turns out, not too many peopel care for the parking here at UB.
Well we've come up with a few ideas that some might care for to make life a bit easier on us all

Sync With the Calender

Know what's going on and where to help find convenient parking! By knowing what's going on around us, we can better find how to deal with the menace that is UB parking.

Integrate with GPS

Use your phones GPS to find the closest and most available parking on campus! By knowing where we are, we can better find spots we cannot see by just looking.

Knowing the Rules

To many times have we been caught off guard! Knowing where we're allowed to park, when we're allowed to park there, and all the rules in between. Filter lots based on parking rules!

Got any ideas?

We're open to suggustion to make this the best it can be! It's for all of us after all
